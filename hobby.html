Hacking

Why do we hack game consoles?

The best way to preserve and enjoy you favorite childhood memories, is to backup the files personally, and play them while you still can.
Nintendo has long since discontinued many of its most beloved consoles and their games alike, so fans like us are the only ones left who solely care about protecting art.

What does the future of retro gaming look like?

Honestly, I have no clue. I would love to say everything will get better but, money rules the world.
Until we reach a day where you can rent the old Super Mario Bros. 2 for an afternoon to relive childhood, I'm just gonna keep on hacking.
